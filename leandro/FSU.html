Overview

Framingham State University is a State College and University that has provided education, majors and minors to people since 1839. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 3,520 (fall 2020), its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 77 acres. The campus of Framingham State University is about 20 miles outside of Boston in Framingham, Massachusetts, it utilizes a semester-based academic calendar.
