Overview

QCC was established in 1963 and for over 55 years, Quinsigamond Community College in Worcester, Massachusetts, has provided thousands of men and women with opportunities for a superior education and personal growth, whether preparing them for immediate employment, transfer to bachelor's degree programs at four-year colleges and universities, or personal and cultural enrichment. Through its Center for Workforce Development and Continuing Education, the College also provides a number of non-credit courses, workshops, and seminars.
