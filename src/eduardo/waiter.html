Waiter

What kind of companies are hiring and what kind of education do you need to work there?

Companies like Ninety-Nine, Apex Entertainment, and IHOP all hire waiters and waitresses in their establishments. They also don't require any education diplomas or degrees.

What are their locations, schedules, and payment?

Restaurants are never usually too sparse, even ones that hire waiters. So restaurants are basically everywhere. Waiters usually work about 8 hours per day if we aren't considering working the evening shift. On average, waiters make about $11 per hour.

What are the qualifications and duties in the workplace?

To be qualified as a waiter, you must have good customer service skills, the ability to work well with others, the ability to accept criticism and work well under pressure, be thorough and pay attention to details, excellent verbal communication skills, a desire to help people, active listening skills, a good memory, and the ability to carry out basic tasks on a computer or hand-held device. Your responsibilities and duties in the restaurant are to greet customers as they arrive and show them to their table, give out menus and take orders for food and drinks, answer questions about menu items and give advice on ingredients, take food orders to kitchen staff, serve food and drinks, make sure customers are happy with their meals and handle any complaints, clear away any used dishes and cutlery from tables, deal with bill payments, and make sure tables are clean and tidy for the next customers.
