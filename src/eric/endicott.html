Overview

Endicott College is a private, four-year college located on Boston’s North Shore. With three private beaches, Endicott’s oceanfront campus in Beverly, Mass., draws a diverse group of undergraduate and graduate students from 30 states and 38 countries across the globe into our vibrant and close-knit community. With three required internships, experiential learning is what sets Endicott apart, giving our students the competitive edge—a degree AND a resume. 90% of Endicott graduates have found employment related to their major and 98% are employed or pursuing an advanced degree.
