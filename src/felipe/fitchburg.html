John and Abigail Adams (Tuition Waiver Scholarship)

This is an in-state tuition, funded by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, the only thing required for this scholarship is a completed FASFA. To keep renewing the scholarship an 3.0 or higher GPA is required.

Henry Frank STEM Scholarship

This is a $2,000 scholarship which is awarded for up to four years for freshmen and up to three years for transfers. To be eligable receive this scholarship you need to be a resident of a town within 35 miles of Orange, MA and a prospective major in one of the STEM degreee programs. To keep renewing the scholarship an 3.0 or higher GPA is required.

Fitchburg State Honors Scholarship

This scholarship can give $2,000 annually for up to four years. You are required to participate in the Honors Program, this is open to in-state and out-of-state applicants. Up to three additional contiguous years of full-time enrollment in the Honors Program with a 3.3 or higher GPA is required for a renewal
