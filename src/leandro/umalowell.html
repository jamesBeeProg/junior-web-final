Scholarship Opportunities

Charles J. HOFF SCHOLARSHIP

The Charles J. Hoff Foundation awards scholarships to full time undergraduate students enrolled in the day program of the College of Fine Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences, Francis College of Engineering, Zuckerberg College of Health Sciences, Kennedy College of Sciences, College of Education or Manning School of Business at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, and to students seeking a bachelor’s degree in the Division of Graduate, Online & Professional Studies. The Hoff Scholarship awards are for $4,000 ($2,000 per semester which will be applied directly to the student’s financial account) and may be continued until graduation on a semester basis, conditional upon satisfactory performance and continued financial aid eligibility. The maximum length of the award is six semesters.

PLTW SCHOLARSHIP

Scholarship requires a nomination from a teacher, principal, etc. Nominations are due by the following dates:
Early Action I: November 5
Early Action II: January 5
Regular Decision: February 5

DEAN'S SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM

This scholarship is limited to a select number of the top 10 percent of our admitted freshmen to attract and reward those with high academic achievements. Annual award up to half of tuition and mandatory fees. Application not required. Admissions application must be received by the Early Action Deadline to be considered for this scholarship. Students are notified via their admissions acceptance packet. Need to maintain a 3.0 GPA.
