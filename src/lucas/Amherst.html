The University of Massachusetts Amherst or UMass Amherst is a public research university located in Massachusetts Amherst. The college was founded in 1863 and is the flagship and largest campus of not only the University of Massachusetts system but also the largest college in the state. The college has an annual enrollment of more than 30,000 students along with approximately 1,300 faculty members.

Computer Science

UMass Amherst provides Bachelors, Masters and Doctoral programs for Computer Science. The Bachelors degree requires you to take 11 computer science courses, three math courses, and a four-course concentration.

Application

The application process for Umass is the same as most colleges which is through common app. Through common app you will provide the following information in your application:

  • Application must be done through common app
  • Highschool transcript
  • Senior Year Grades
  • Essay
  • Letter of recommendation
  • Standardized Tests (Optional)

SAT

Reading & Writing: 550-630


Math: 540-610
