Boston University is a non sectarian private institute located in Boston Massachusetts. The college was founded in 1839 and its original campus was located in Newbury, Vermont before moving to Boston in 1867. The University boasts a 20% acceptance rate and a 87% graduation rate.

Computer Science

Boston University's Computer Science is within the top 10% of the country. Offering Bachelors, Masters and PHD programs for those who manage to pursue that career path.

Tuition and other Expenses

Tuition for Boston University as well as other costs related to the school have been rising in recent years. Tuition alone is $61,050 however costs such as room, dining plans and other expenses bring up the cost up to $82,760
