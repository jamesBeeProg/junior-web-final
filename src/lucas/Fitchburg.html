Fitchburg State University is a public university located in Fitchburg Massachusetts. Founded in 1894 by the Massachusetts General Court the university offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in 25 academic disciplines.

Computer Science

Fitchburg State offers only a Bachelors degree in computer science. The program has students work in areas such as software design, network development, web design, and computer hardware. As well as develop some knowledge and skills related to business information and application.

Tour Dates

On campus open houses are said to open in the fall. However individual visits can be scheduled on the Fitchburg State website. If you choose to not take a in person tour Fitchburg state offers a virtual tour

Application

The application process for Fitchburg State is the same as most colleges which is through common app. Through common app you will provide the following information in your application:

  • Application must be done through common app
  • Highschool transcript
  • Senior Year Grades
  • Essay
  • Letter of recommendation
  • Standardized Tests (Optional)

SAT

UMass Amherst is test optional for the class of 2023. Historically the typical SAT scores are as follows:


Reading & Writing: 550-630


Math: 540-610
